10 Guys We Totally Forgot Kim Kardashian Dated

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

There is no doubt that the world can’t seem to get enough of Kim Kardashian — after all the reality television star has been in the eye of the public since Keeping Up With the Kardashians premiered in 2007.

Related: 10 Things Kim Kardashian and Kanye West’s Employees Have Said About Working For Them

When it comes to Kim’s love life fans are always interested in knowing more but in 2020 most of us can think of only a few of Kim's ex-boyfriends — mainly Reggie Bush (because of the infamous tape) and Kris Humphries (because of their 72 days of marriage). Of course, everyone knows that Kim has been married to Kanye West since 2014 but who was Kim dating before the famous rapper?

10 Nick Lachey

Kicking the list off is actor and musician Nick Lachey whom plenty of millennials remember as a member of the 90s boyband 98 Degrees as well as singer Jessica Simpson‘s ex-husband.

Kim Kardashian and Nick Lachey actually briefly dated in 2006 after his split from Jessica, and in an interview on watch Watch What Happens Live With Andy Cohen the actor even revealed that the two went on a date to see The Da Vinci Code.

9 50 Cent

Next on the list is rapper 50 Cent who was rumored to be dating Kim Kardashian in August of 2009. At the time, the two stars were frequently seen together at different star-studded events and they certainly always seemed very flirtatious with each other.

Of course, as fans of the reality television star already know, Kim eventually went for another famous rapper meaning that 50 Cent just remained one of her famous exes we all forgot about!

8 Nick Cannon

Another famous face that used to date Kim Kardashian is comedian Nick Cannon. Nick and Kim dated from September 2006 to January 2007, and yes — the sharp eye will notice that during that time the reality television star was also linked to Nick Lachey and 50 Cent.

In an interview on The Howard Stern Show, Nick Cannon confessed that he actually broke up with Kim Kardashian because she lied about a certain infamous tape that was leaked...

7 Cristiano Ronaldo

The only athlete that made it onto this list is Portuguese professional soccer player Cristiano Ronaldo — most people remember Kim Kardashian's short-lived marriage to basketball player Kris Humphries.

Related: 10 Pics Showing The Evolution Of Paris Hilton And Kim Kardashian’s Friendship

While there are no photos of Kim and Cristiano together, the two were linked to each other back in 2010, right before Kim started dating Kris Humphries. Allegedly things got quite steamy during Kim's vacation in Madrid, where the soccer player was living at the time.

6 The Game

Another famous rapper who is also one of Kim Kardashian's exes is The Game. The two stars were linked to each other back in 2006, and just like with 50 Cent — Kim was also frequently seen hanging out with The Game at different events.

While the reality television star never confirmed her relationship with the rapper, he frequently stated that the two were more than just friends — and he even rapped about Kim K in one of his songs!

5 Gabriel Aubry

In 2010 Kim Kardashian was dating Canadian model Gabriel Aubrey who had just gotten separated from Hollywood star Halle Berry. After the two stars were spotted cozying up courtside at a basketball game it was pretty evident that Kim and Gabriel were a thing.

Unfortunately, their relationship didn’t last for too long as they broke up after only two months — allegedly because Halle Berry wasn't too happy about their romance!

4 Benji Madden

Next on the list is yet another musician — this time we're talking about guitarist and singer Benji Madden. Benji is another famous face that Kim has been linked to, however, there are no photos of the two together.

Related: 10 Inspiring Posts From Kim Kardashian You Have To See

The reality television star and the Good Charlotte member briefly dated in February 2007 but it seems as if the two soon realized that they’re not a perfect match — and both of them quickly moved on!

3 Evan Ross

Another famous face that Kim Kardashian dated back in 2007 is Diana Ross' son, actor Evan Ross. Kim and Evan dated for two months in 2007 — from May to July — and even though they were frequently seen together it seems as if the two couldn’t make it last for too long.

The reality television star, who was in her twenties at the time, certainly didn't have any trouble moving as she was quickly afterward officially dating former football player Reggie Bush.

2 Michael Copon

Hardcore fans of Keeping Up With the Kardashians probably remember the face of Michael Copon as the star actually went out on a date with Kim Kardashian on the show's spin-off Kim and Kourtney Take New York in 2010.

For those who haven’t seen the spin-off but Michael’s face still seems familiar — the actor is best known for his roles in the television shows One Tree Hill and Beyond the Break.

1 Bow Wow

Wrapping up the list of Kim K's exes most of us have forgotten about is yet another rapper — this time we're talking about Bow Wow.

The reality television star and Bow Wow allegedly dated back in 2007 and while Kim Kardashian never confirmed the rumors, Bow Wow — or formally known as Lil' Bow Wow – claims that he dated Kim and that it was actually Kim's BFF at the time, Paris Hilton, that connected the two!
