2 Chainz Biography; Net Worth, Age, Real Name, Kids, Degree, Songs, Album And Wife

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 1, 2024

2 Chainz known originally as Tauheed Epps is an American rapper, songwriter, media personality, and a basketball player. 2 Chainz was born on 12 September 1977 at College Park, Georgia, in the United States of America.

2 Chainz attended North Clayton High School where he played basketball. He later proceeded to Alabama State University on Scholarship after graduating with excellence result from high school. While in the University, 2 Chainz played in the basketball team from 1995 to 1997.

2 Chainz started his music career in 1997 where he formed a hip hop duo with Playaz circle in College Park in Georgia, U.S.A. 2 Chainz was called Tity Boi when he started his career as a musician. He later changed his name from Tity Boi to 2 Chainz in 2011. 2 Chainz gained popularity when he changed his name and has released a lot of award-winning songs.

2 Chainz Net Worth

2 Chainz has an estimated net worth of $9 million. He makes his money through his music career, TV career, endorsement deals, and other businesses running for him.

2 Chainz Real Name

2 Chainz as his stage name has overshadowed his real name as many only know him by his stage name. However, 2 Chainz’s real name is Tauheed Epps.

2 Chainz Kids

2 Chainz is happily married and is blessed with three kids. He is having two sons, Harmony Epps and Halo Epps, and a daughter, Heaven Epps.

2 Chainz Wife

2 Chainz is happily married to Nakesha Ward, better known as Kesha, his long time girlfriend. 2 Chainz and Nakesha Ward tied the marriage knot on 18 August 2018.

2 Chainz Degree

2 Chainz attended Alabama State University where he obtained a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology
