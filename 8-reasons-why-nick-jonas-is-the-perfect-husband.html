8 Reasons Why Nick Jonas Is The Perfect Husband

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 20, 2024

Despite our hearts being a little broken when Nick Jonas was finally "off the market", we are all so happy to see his beautiful marriage to Priyanka Chopra. They are definitely one of the most well known couples in Hollywood. Also, they recently announced that they are welcoming their first child via surrogate. The couple started dating in early 2018, and have been inseparable ever since. They got engaged only after a month of being together. It has been clear since then that they are deeply in love with each other. Keep scrolling to find out what makes Nick Jonas a great husband.

8 His Engagement Ring

Nick Jonas did not spare anything when selecting the perfect engagement ring for the love of his life. The ring is what anyone would want their future life partner to pick for them. He chose a $200,000 diamond ring to propose while the couple was in Crete, Greece. It is almost unbelievable how stunning it is.

7 He Makes The First Move

As beautiful as Priyanka Chopra is, it makes sense why someone might be too intimidated to ask her out. However, not only did Nick Jonas make the first move by sliding into her DMs, he was the first to propose. He does not hesitate, and that makes him the perfect husband.

6 He Honors His Wife's Culture

While this may seem like the bare minimum, some husbands allow their wife's culture and upbringing to fall second to theirs. Nick Jonas chose to celebrate Priyanka Chopra's culture. They even had a traditional Hindu wedding with all of their close friends and family.

5 He Knows How To Slow Down

Nick Jonas has allowed his marriage to Chopra to teach him to be present. He has learned to slow down and enjoy the little things that life has to offer. This has allowed his marriage to flourish, and it makes him a great husband.

4 He Prioritizes His Family

Nick Jonas has shown since the start of his relationship to Priyanka Chopra that he is a family man, through and through. This is a great quality to have as a husband, especially with their new baby in mind. He is constantly working on being a good husband, son, and father so that he can put his family first.

3 He Is Ambitious

One of the things that actually connected Jonas and Chopra was their passion for their work. Nowadays, he does everything he can to pursue his dreams. It is a great quality for a husband to be able to chase after their ambitions, and Priyanka loves her husband for it.

2 He Is Always Supportive

Along with chasing his own dreams, Nick Jonas wants his wife to achieve hers. They both have a fiery passion for their work, and Jonas does everything he can to fuel his wife's fire. It is almost like a fairytale how much Nick Jonas supports Priyanka, and he is a great husband for it.

1 He Values Quality Time Over Everything

Nick Jonas knows how important it is for a husband and wife to spend quality time together. They actually spent most of the pandemic attached at the hip. It is also rare to see either of them anywhere without the other. It is a great quality for a husband to push for time to spend with his wife.
