AEW Revolution 2023 Date And Venue Confirmed

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 25, 2024

will be held in California in 2023.

As confirmed by AEW President Tony Khan during the AEW Full Gear post-show media scrum, the pay-per-view will emanate from the Chase Center in San Francisco, California, for March 5, 2023. This will mark the first time Revolution is held on the West Coast; Chicago hosted the inaugural event, as it was held at the Wintrust Arena in 2019. When the COVID-19 pandemic shut down touring, AEW held Revolution from its home base, Daily’s Place in Jacksonville, Florida, in 2021. The following year, Addition Financial Field in Orlando, Florida, housed Revolution.

Fightful previously reported that San Francisco was being considered for the 2023 version of the show, and now it’s officially the host city.
