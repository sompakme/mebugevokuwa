Early Life

Andre Lauren Benjamin, better known as Andre 3000, was born on May 27, 1975, in Atlanta, Georgia. Raised by a single mother, Andre spent his childhood in various parts of Georgia, developing a deep connection to the local culture and music scene.

During his high school years, fate brought Andre together with Antwan Patton, who would later become his lifelong collaborator in the iconic hip-hop duo, Outkast. Little did they know that this partnership would shape their destinies and transform the face of rap music.

In 1994, Outkast released their debut album, “Southernplayalisticadillacmuzik,” which quickly achieved platinum status, solidifying their position as one of the most influential rap groups of the time.

Throughout their career, Outkast continued to captivate audiences with their diverse sounds and innovative style.
