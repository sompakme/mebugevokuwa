Chris Hemsworth's Net Worth: How Thor Earns And Spends His $130 Million Fortune

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Highlights

  • Chris Hemsworth's net worth is estimated at $130 million, earned in large part through his role in Thor films and the MCU.
  • Hemsworth's income streams include acting in blockbusters, brand endorsements, and co-owning a production company.

This article explores Hemsworth's complex financial world, goes into the different income streams which add to his wealth, and the many ways he indulges in his riches.
