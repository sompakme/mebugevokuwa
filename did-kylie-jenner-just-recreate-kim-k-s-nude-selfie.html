Did Kylie Jenner Just Recreate Kim K's Nude Selfie?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

?

A post shared by Kylie (@kyliejenner) on Mar 9, 2016 at 11:22am PST

Well, this looks familiar!

After tweeting her support of big sister Kim Kardashian's nude selfie, Kylie Jenner took her own turn showing off her body in a very similar pic, posted to Instagram on Wednesday, March 9.

The only difference? Jenner's actually wearing clothes — a bikini to be exact. The black bandeau covering her chest looks a lot like the Selfish author's censor bars, and even the swimsuit's bottom evokes the same feeling.

After Kardashian received backlash for her throwback snap, Jenner, 18, showed her solidarity. "Kim is a legend," she wrote on March 7.

When you're like I have nothing to wear LOL pic.twitter.com/UlSLZb1fp1

— Kim Kardashian West (@KimKardashian) March 7, 2016

Though Chloë Grace Moretz, Bella Thorne, Chrissy Teigen, Bette Midler, Piers Morgan and many others have voiced support and criticism for the image, Kardashian, 35, dedicated an entire blog post to it, explaining that it makes her proud. 

"I don't do drugs, I hardly drink, I've never committed a crime — and yet I'm a bad role model for being proud of my body?" she wrote in response. "I am empowered by my body. I am empowered by my sexuality. I am empowered by feeling comfortable in my skin. I am empowered by showing the world my flaws and not being afraid of what anyone is going to say about me. And I hope that through this platform I have been given, I can encourage the same empowerment for girls and women all over the world."
