Dont Worry About My Pockets

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Roc-A-Fella Records co-founder Dame Dash is a man about his paper. After reports that Dame owed $2 million in taxes and allegedly abandoned his upstate New York mansion surfaced last year, the hip-hop mogul says no man should talk about another man’s pockets.

“First of all, how could I be broke and I got all these companies?,” he told Hip Hop Motivation Corporation. “Second of all, why the fuck are people talking about another man’s pocket. That means that’s a general problem in the Black community. Too worried about another niggas’ pockets…Worrying about another man’s pocket is not reflective of a real man’s perspective. Real men do not give a fuck about how much another man got. It’s incomprehensible for me to her another dude talk about another’s dude’s pockets and have that matter and argue about that.”

He also discussed how he invests his money and blacks selling their celebrity and not their skill. Watch the full interview above.
