Drew McIntyre Costs Sami Zayn Match Against Shinsuke Nakamura On WWE RAW

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 5, 2024

Sami Zayn was left looking for answers at the end of the February 12 episode of WWE RAW.

On Monday night, Sami Zayn hoped to get back on track with a win over Shinsuke Nakamura. In recent weeks, Zayn has come up short in the Royal Rumble, and he failed to qualify for the Elimination Chamber, leaving his road to WrestleMania unclear. However, Drew McIntyre threw a wrench in Zayn’s plans, as he distracted Sami long enough for Nakamura to blast him with a Kinshasha for the win.

Zayn’s night was about to get worse with a post-match attack by McIntyre and Nakamura. Thankfully for Zayn, Cody Rhodes fought them off. Still, disappointment was written all over his face at the end of the show.
