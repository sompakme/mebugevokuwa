Fans Think There's A Scary Reason Megan Fox Hasn't Dumped Machine Gun Kelly Yet

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Even though they might have pressed 'pause,' Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly are known to have an on/off relationship, full of passion and drama. Since they were first spotted together in 2020, they weren't afraid to show off their affection in public.

From steamy makeout sessions to matching tattoos, these two are all about the PDA. This is the same case when it comes to their arguments as both go to social media to either deny rumors, delete pictures with the other, or even hint at cheating. Then, as if nothing happened, they are back together, showing off their affection for each other during events or on social media.

But controversy and drama still emerge from their love story, no matter how much they try to assure everyone that things are good. Some people claim that their relationship is 'toxic,' while others found even scarier reasons why Megan Fox hasn't dumped Machine Gun Kelly definitely yet.

Megan Fox And Machine Gun Kelly Seem To Have An On/Off Relationship

Are they together or not? This is probably one of the most Googled questions regarding Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly's relationship. From their steamy romance to their shocking splits, the couple has kept their fans guessing about the state of their relationship.

One day they declare their undying love for each other on social media and the next day it seems like they just want to stay away from each other. Considering this chaotic timeline, fans still think that, no matter the status of their relationship, they will break up completely at some point.

As they work on their relationship after they reportedly broke up, it seems that people still found some pretty scary reasons why they are still working on it and don't just call it off completely.

Megan Fox Hinted At MGK's Infidelity But Then Denied It's Real

After one of their arguments, Megan Fox made some shocking decisions when it comes to her social media, even closing her account for some time. Still, before she did that, she let the world know that there is trouble in paradise. Even when this incident happened, people weren't sure if they broke up definitely.

After they partied the night before, Megan Fox deleted all photos with Machine Gun Kelly on her social media. While this action made it seem that they had problems, she also added a selfie in the bathroom, captioning it "You can taste the dishonesty/it’s all over your breath," which comes from Beyoncé's song about infidelity.

However, Sophie Lloyd responded to the allegations and denied any rumors shortly after. Even Megan Fox came back to Instagram to do the same, but fans still don't understand the meaning of her lyrics.

Fans Are Worried About Megan Fox's Well-Being

Fast-forward to one year later, Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly still work on their relationship. However, fans think she kind of wants to be out of it, but for a certain scary reason, ending things with him isn't that simple.
