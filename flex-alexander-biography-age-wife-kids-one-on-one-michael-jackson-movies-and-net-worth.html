Who is Flex Alexander? | Flex Alexander Biography- Wiki

Flex Alexander is an American actor, comedian and dancer who was born and raised in Harlem, New York, United States. Currently, he is known for playing a role in the comedy series called One on One. At first, Flex developed an interest in dancing as a teenager in New York night-clubs. There he was able to earn the nickname Flex due to his acrobatic dance skills.

Flex was then discovered by DJ Spinderella and he began to work with Salt-n-Pepa, a rap trio. He was also the one who choreographed for Mary J, Blige and Queen Latifah in their music videos. Flex then made his stand-up comedy debut in 1989 making his fame rise. Eventually, he decided to pursue acting as a full-time career. His first film debut was in the 1992 film called Juice and became a regular cast member of the sketch comedy show, Uptown Comedy Club. The show ran from 1992-1994.
