Gage vs. Bonnar Signed For Threat Level Midnight, Dan Ryckert Confirms New WWE Position

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Gage vs. Bonnar Signed For Threat Level Midnight

Black Label Pro has announced that Nick Gage and Stephan Bonnar will be in a match at their Wrestlemania week showcase, titled Threat Level Midnight. Taking place at The Cuban Club in Ybor City, these two deathmatch legends are sure to deliver something uniquely spectacular as the clock strikes midnight.

Dan Ryckert Confirms New WWE Position

Dan Ryckert, a video games writer most recently employed at Giant Bomb, has confirmed that he is leaving the gaming industry to become a podcast producer at WWE. Dan has had many run-ins with wrestlers during his time in games, including a long-running rivalry with New Day member Xavier Woods.

Dan tweeted out to his followers about the new job, saying that he was moving from one dream job to another.

So if you want to follow my work (albeit in a behind the scenes role this time), subscribe to After the Bell and The New Day: Feel the Power. Can't wait to get to Stamford and get started!

— Dan Ryckert (@DanRyckert) January 3, 2020

Meanwhile, Xavier also commented on the move, seemingly happy to have his frenemy close at hand.

Dan you now belong to us. Embrace the most stressful but fulfilling job you will ever have. Let us begin…. https://t.co/AnTNsfX1N2 pic.twitter.com/Kq0MHQECHn

If you’ve heard of Dan Ryckert before but can’t quite place him, you may have seen something about his marriage, which took place at a Taco Bell. WWE is truly getting a unique individual to lead their podcasting endeavors.
