The National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA)

The National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) is the largest nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting individuals and families affected by eating disorders. They provide resources, information, and support networks to those who are struggling with these complex mental health conditions.

NEDA offers helpline services, educational programs, advocacy initiatives, and community outreach to raise awareness about eating disorders and promote early intervention and access to treatment. Their goal is to create a world where all individuals have a positive body image and a healthy relationship with food.

Hannah Brown’s partnership with NEDA allows her to contribute to their mission and reach a wider audience with their important messages. Together, they strive to break the stigma surrounding eating disorders and encourage individuals to seek help and support.
