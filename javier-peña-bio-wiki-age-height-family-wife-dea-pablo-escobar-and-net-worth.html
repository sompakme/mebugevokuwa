Javier Peña Biography

Javier Peña (born) Javier F. Peña (born 1958) is a retired American DEA agent who worked with his former colleague Stephen Murphy and the Colombian National Police to investigate Pablo Escobar and the Medelln Cartel. Peña was a consultant on the Netflix show Narcos. Peña was not involved in the DEA’s Cali Cartel operations; his involvement in the investigation depicted in Season 3 of the Netflix series is a fictitious account. Following the investigation of the Medelln Cartel, Peña joined the DEA and worked in Puerto Rico, Texas, and Colombia. In 2014, Peña retired from the DEA.

Peña worked as a Deputy Sheriff for the Webb County Sheriff’s Office in Laredo from 1977 to 1984, and then with the DEA until his retirement in January 2014. He co-wrote Manhunters: How We Took Down Pablo Escobar with Steve Murphy in 2019.

He is one of the main characters in the Netflix TV series Narcos, which has three seasons and is portrayed by Pedro Pascal. Peña served as a consultant on the show. He makes a cameo appearance alongside Murphy in Season 2’s final episode, “Al Fin Cayó!”
