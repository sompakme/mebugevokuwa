Jessica Simpson Slurs, Seems Dizzy During HSN Appearance -- Watch!

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Are you feeling okay, Jessica?! Jessica Simpson caused a Twitter storm during her Thursday, Sept. 17 appearance on the Home Shopping Network as fans jumped on social media to question her bizarre, confused behavior and slurring of words. Click here to see Jessica's steamy Instagram photos.
