Kate Gosselin Appeals Courts Decision To Deny $133,000 In Child Support

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Highlights

  • Kate Gosselin is appealing a court's decision regarding unpaid child support from her ex-husband Jon, despite the case being dismissed in November.
  • Jon's representative claims that Kate's appeal is a desperate attempt to make money during difficult times and suggests that she should get a job.
  • Jon has accused Kate of stealing from their children's trust funds, which she denies, and the couple continues to argue over financial matters despite their children being nearly legal adults.

Over a decade after their divorce, Kate Gosselin is still trying to get child support from her ex-husband Jon Gosselin, even though a court threw out her request earlier this year.

According to court documents obtained by Ok! Magazine, the former reality star is appealing the court’s decision that Jon doesn’t owe her $132,000 in back due child support. Kate initially began fighting her ex for the money in 2018, but a court dismissed the case in November. However, Kate went on to appeal the decision, claiming she is owed the money.

A representative for Jon told the media that the father-of-eight was looking forward to being done with the legal battle, but it appears he’s still going to be seeing his ex-wife in court thanks to her appeal. But the rep claims it’s merely a desperate attempt for Kate to make money in difficult times.

"Unfortunately, Kate can’t seem to let this go despite the judge’s decisions. This doesn’t look good for her. I’m sure the judges won’t take kindly to this," the rep claimed. "She is free now that all her children are in college. This seems like just another very desperate move to make money. I think it’s time for Kate to get a job."

Kate Gosselin and her daughters 

Although Kate believes she’s owed child support, Jon has accused her of stealing from their children’s trust funds. He claims she withdrew $50,000 on two separate occasions, though Kate later defended herself, saying it was for necessary living costs.

“You can’t just live off your kids’ money,” he told The Sun . “It makes me feel disgusted.”

“She has been on TV telling the world she has saved her children’s money when she’s actually been stealing from them-– she’s a hypocrite,” he went on, calling his ex’s actions “morally wrong.”

Even though Jon and Kate continue to argue over money, all of their eight children are nearly legal adults. Their oldest kids, twin daughters Maddy and Cara, have been living out of the house for years after beginning college. Four of the former couple’s sextuplets live with Kate, while the other two live with Jon and apparently have limited contact with their mom. Kate’s current net worth hasn’t been verified, but reports suggest it could be as high as $500,000 when considering all of her assets.
