Kim Domingo Biography

Kim Domingo is a Filipina actress, model and internet personality. She is also a popular television presenter and for playing the leading antagonist in Juan Happy Love Story, Tsuperhero and Super Ma’am. Kim is currently a GMA Network contract actress and artist.

Furthermore, she was also born as an only child to a French father and a Filipino mother. However, Kim possesses the surname of her mother because her parents did not marry and they are currently living separately. Kim used to attend elementary school at the San Agustin Elementary School in Quezon City.

Kim described herself as a shy pupil who always kept quiet in school and stayed alone. From there she proceeded to Our Lady of Fatima University where she pursued dentistry. Domingo first rose to fame with her dubsmash of the song Twerk it like Miley in 2014. This song earned her fame and made her sign a contract with GMA Network’s talent agency.

In 2015, Kim began to bag roles on Juan Happy Love Story and Bubble Gang. Later on, Domingo also appeared on the cover of FHM Philippines December 2015 and January 2017 issues. Kim then signed her first recording contract with GMA Records in August 2016. This was where she released her debut single Know Me featuring international rapper C-Tru. From there, Kim was introduced by Ginebra San Miguel as their 2017 calendar girl in October 2016.
