Kylie Jenner Adds A $50K Birkin Bag &amp; More To Her Million-Dollar Closet

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Kylie Jenner did a bit of shopping recently, as she revealed on social media that she’s added new accessories to her wardrobe.

According to Daily Mail, the makeup mogul shared an image of her new crocodile print Hermes Birkin bag, valued at $50,000, to Instagram. Also featured in the photo was a pair of $2,000 Givenchy heels. The new accessories were sitting on shelving in Kylie’s million-dollar closet, surrounded by her other shoes and bags.

“Hello fall,” the reality star captioned the photo, seeming to imply she purchased the new items to spruce up her wardrobe for the fall season.

It’s been reported for years that Kylie’s closet is worth well over a million dollars, which isn’t surprising given her billionaire status.

Elle reports that the businesswoman owns at least five Hermes Birkin and Kelly handbags – two of the priciest designer bags on the market. Some of the most expensive bags in Kylie’s collection are estimated to be valued at approximately $300,000.

The outlet suggests that her handbags alone are worth half a million dollars. This may explain why Kylie needs an entirely separate room to house her handbag collection, which is often seen on her social medias. Other estimates suggest it could be an easy $1 million.

Kylie also has some exceptionally expensive clothing pieces in her wardrobe. A custom Balmain dress she wore to her 23rd birthday cost a whopping $24,595, while a feathery Oscar de la Renta gown she rocked for her 22nd birthday was a cool $22,000.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder’s most expensive outfit to date may be what she wore to the 2019 Met Gala. Dressed in a custom Versace gown and 15-carat Lorraine Schwartz diamond jewellery valued at $4.1 million, her entire look cost $8.6 million.

Kylie also has an affinity for accessories, as evidenced by her recent purchases. Additionally, one of her most famous jewellery pieces is a large, pear-shaped diamond rings. Estimates have suggested the rock is worth anywhere between $750,000 to $1 million.

It’s not only Kylie’s wardrobe that’s impressive. She’s also invested a pretty penny in her daughter Stormi’s clothing collection. The 3-year old has been spotted in brands like Burberry, Louis Vuitton, and Hermés since she was a young infant.

One source told Life & Style that Kylie regularly drops $100,000 per week on her daughter, which includes many lavish gifts like “expensive jewelry [and] the latest gadgets.”

Kylie announced in September that she’s expecting another child with Travis Scott. Given her affinity for designer duds, we’re sure Kylie will have fun filling her second baby’s closet.
