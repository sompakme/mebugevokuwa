Lee Hyori was born on 10th May 1979. She is a South Korean singer with an estimated net worth of over $18 million. Lee has accumulated this wealth over the years by being a hip hop and a pop singer for various bands. She was born in the South Korean town of Cheongwon and started her career in music from an early age. She also endorses many products for businesses including the two big brands Samsung and Adidas. Lee’s wealth comes from these endorsements as well.

Hyori came to the spotlight with her debut in the all-girl Korean group “Fin K.L” which went on to become the most popular group in Korea, even overtaking “S.E.S”. After performing with her band for four years, she released her solo album in the end of 2003 titled “Stylish E”. Over the years, she did many performances with her band before signing a big contract with M-Net entertainment. This made her the highest paid singer in all of Korea. She also performed an R&B number for the music “If in Love Like Them”. Lee also hosts many television shows such as “Happy Together” and “Time Machine” during the time between her albums. It was during this time that she released her third album on 16th July 2008 titled “Its Hyorish”. It climbed the charts and made a worldwide record. It gave her worldwide fame.

Lee has been in a relationship with musician Lee Sang soon since 2011. She is also an active supporter of animal rights and welfare, and maintains a strict and healthy vegetarian diet.
