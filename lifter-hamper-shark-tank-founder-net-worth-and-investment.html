The Story of Lifter Hamper and the Founder Marvin Philip

Marvin Philip, a former NFL player, is the visionary behind the revolutionary Lifter Hamper. His personal experience with a back injury sustained during his football career inspired him to create a solution that would make laundry organization easier and more efficient.

Marvin recognized the struggle of bending over to pick up clothes from the bottom of a traditional hamper, which often exacerbated his back pain. Determined to find a better way, he invested $75,000 of his own money to develop the Lifter Hamper’s patented design.

The Lifter Hamper features a unique bungee spring-loaded mechanism that lifts clothes to the top of the hamper as items are added. This innovative design eliminates the need to bend over, reducing strain on the back and making laundry organization a breeze. Although Marvin didn’t secure a deal on “Shark Tank,” the exposure from the show catapulted the Lifter Hamper into the spotlight. The product quickly sold out, validating Marvin’s vision and prompting him to run successful crowdfunding campaigns to meet the increased demand. The Lifter Hamper has since become a household name, recognized for its ingenious design and practicality.

Lifter Hamper’s Shark Tank Pitch and Investment Outcome
