Margaret Orr Meteorologist at WDSU

Prior to joining WDSU, she worked in Charleston, South Carolina as a weather forecaster. Since then, Orr began forecasting weather at 5, 6 and 10 p.m. after many years on the morning news. She was promoted to chief meteorologist after the semi-retirement of Dan Milham in 2019.

Orr became a co-host of the Breakfast Edition with Bill Stanley, and later co-host of the “World’s Fair Show with Alec Gifford during the 1984 Louisiana World Exposition. For more than 20 years, Orr co-hosted the Children’s Hospital Telethon with Norman Robinson helping to raise millions of dollars for Children’s Hospital.
