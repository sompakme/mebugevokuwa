This Tribute Was Posted Shortly After Rumors About Her Relationship Status Swirled... Again

Power recently starred in the latest season of Celebs Go Dating, alongside Married at First Sight Alum Nikita Jasmine. This famous reality show is about stars who live together, while the Celebrity Dating Agency in London works in setting them up on blind dates with people outside the entertainment industry. Various celebrities have taken part in this show, including Stephanie Pratt, Jonathan Lipnicki, and Melody Thornton. Although Power was a late addition to the season cast, controversies quickly arose for her.

During the show, it was revealed that she was already in a relationship with Thompson before filming. A source from the series told The Sun, "Jessika told bosses she was completely single when she signed the contract, insisting all rumours she was dating Connor were false and that she really wanted the agency's help finding a British boyfriend."

The source continued to discuss what Power had said to friends and her real intentions toward the show. "She told pals she only agreed to do the show as it was a huge opportunity that'd boost her name in the UK. She moved to England for Connor and never had any interest in the guys the agency had lined up for her."
