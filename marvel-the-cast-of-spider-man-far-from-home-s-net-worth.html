Marvel: The Cast Of Spider-Man: Far From Home's Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Quick Links

  • Tom Holland - $4 Million
  • Zendaya Coleman - $5 Million
  • Jake Gyllenhaal - $65 Million
  • Samuel L. Jackson - $220 Million
  • Jacob Batalon - $1 Million
  • Marisa Tomei - $20 Million
  • Jon Favreau - $60 Million
  • J. K. Simmons - $16 Million

Spider-Man: Far From Home became the first Spider-Man movie to hit $1 billion marks. According to The Hollywood Reporter, this would be the second Sony movie to join the billion-dollar league. The movie was directed by Jon Watts, who also co-directed and co-wrote the horror film Clown.

The Spidey's sequel achieved the amount of $333 million in the US and $672 million overseas becoming one of Marvel Cinematic Universe's (MCU) most memorable success. So with that in mind, let's dig into the net worth of the cast of the film from the titular wall-crawler to the mythical menace Mysterio.

Tom Holland - $4 Million

The British actor made his debut as Spider-Man in the Marvel Universe in 2016. Nevertheless, before his big principal role as a hero, he previously worked with the superstar Naomi Watts in the disaster drama filmed The Impossible.  Playing the role of Lucas Bennett, the 12 years old son that is in search of his family after the catastrophe alongside her mom.

The 23 years old actor of Peter Parker officially jumped to fame thanks to the Marvel icon. According to Seventeen's Magazine, the first movie of the classic hero earned more than $880 million. So far his net worth of $4 million and with coming projects, the numbers will increase for sure.

Zendaya Coleman - $5 Million

The multi-talented actress, dancer, singer, and model Zendaya started her career as a Disney Channel's star starring the role of Rocky Blue in Shake It Up and K.C. Cooper in K.C Undercover. She also appears in the successful musical The Greatest Show Man as Anne Wheeler, an acrobat and trapeze artist that is the love interest of Phillip Carlyle played by Zac Efron.

Zendaya even stole most of the screentime of Bruno Mars' song Versace On The Floor music video. Being well known among the younger audience, casting her for the Spider-Man series was a smart decision. According to FilmZone, her net worth is estimated at $5 million, this total includes remunerations from movies, music career, and many projects. Also, Coleman owns a $1.4 million house in Los Angeles.

Jake Gyllenhaal - $65 Million

Gyllenhaal started his career in his childhood being part of City Slickers, his very first movie. However, as the actor got older he started being cast for major roles such as Dastan in the Walt Disney Pictures and Jerry Bruckheimer Films production Prince Of Persia: The Sands Of Time.

The film based in a video game collected $355 million. Filmzone current stats calculate a net worth around $65 million for the actor, claiming that he is making a solid income thanks to his acting projects.

Samuel L. Jackson - $220 Million

The iconic Marvel character Nick Fury is brought to life by the brilliant actor Samuel L. Jackson, who is well known for playing badass roles.  He has even been considered by some as the most important character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

From the storytelling treasures Pulp Fiction and Star Wars, Wealthy Gorilla throws the mind-blowing figure of $220 Million for the actor. Every cent earned has been well deserved.

Jacob Batalon - $1 Million

Batalon's actual earnings vary from $1 to $5 Million according to Trend Celebs Now. Playing Peter's best friend and partner make him one of the most important supporting cast members. The 23 years old actor (same age as Tom Holland and Zendaya!) caught Spider-Man producer's attention with his role of Cooper in the 2016 film North Woods.

Giving him a chance to persuade his acting dreams, the young star starts making cameos and appearances after Spider-Man: Homecoming release in films like Every Day and Blood Fest. Batalon is walking cautiously but with a strong presence in this industry. He has also shown his comedy skills during Marvel's promotion for Spider-Man.

Marisa Tomei - $20 Million

No one looks hotter past their 50s except for Spider-Man's aunt actress Marisa Tomei. The charismatic beauty has an estimated net worth of $20 million according to Top Planet Info. Can you imagine Aunt May dating Iron Man? Well, both stars dated after meeting in Chaplin and Only You sets.

They broke up at that time, between 1992 and 1994, until they were seen again on screen in Captain America: Civil War and Spider-Man: Homecoming movies. It's a small world!

Jon Favreau - $60 Million

Happy Hogan' adorable love story with Spider-Man's aunt is a nice break from drama through the film. Peter Parker finds great support in him. During Far From Home, both characters' interaction grows and flourishes. It is affirmed that after Tony Stark's passing, Happy becomes Peter's closest mentor. NetworthBuzz assures that Favreau's current income is close to $60 million.

As a curious fact, the star worked not only as an actor but as a director alongside Robert Downey Jr. for the first two Iron Man movies. Also, the Spider-Man cast member directed the cinematographic piece winner of an Oscar for Best Achievement in Visual Effects in 2017 for his movie The Jungle Book.

J. K. Simmons - $16 Million

Making an appearance at the end of Far From Home as J. Jonah Jameson, the newspaper editor who is ready to reveal Spider-Man's true identity. The winning actor of an Academy Award, the British Academy of Film and Television Arts (BAFTA), the Golden Globe and the Screen Actors Guild Awards have a stunning career with additional highlights such as performing on Broadway.

All this success has given him an actual income of $16 million according to Celebrity Net Worth. Simmons is famous for his role as Terrence Fletcher in the acclaimed hit movie Whiplash. As an interesting fact in honor of Spider-Man's latest movie, Sony created a real website for The Daily Bugle featuring Simmons' character in the film.
