Panda Restaurant Group

In 1982, Peggy Cherng joined Panda Restaurant Group, the company founded by her husband and his father. She played a key role in developing the company’s computer systems, which helped track customer feedback and streamline operations. Over the years, the Cherngs expanded the franchise, owning and operating over 2,200 Panda Express locations by 2019. They also acquired stakes in other restaurant franchises such as Urbane Cafe, Just Salad, Uncle Tetsu, Pieology, and Ippudo.
