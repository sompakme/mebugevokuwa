Peggy Tanous is a professional spokesmodel, former model and a cast member of “The Real Housewives of Orange County” who has an estimated net worth of -$3 million.

Peggy was a bikini model and an infomercial host and spokesperson for Talent 6, which hires people to be extras in movies, commercials, and music videos. She has also been the spokeswoman for her husband Micah’s website business, My Virtual Salesperson.com. She was born and raised in Orange County, California and has four sisters.

She is married to the internet developer and CEO of Monarchy Holdings, Micah Tanous. The couple has two daughters, Capri and London. She refused previous offers to join the show, “The Real Housewives of Orange County,” but eventually joined it in the sixth season to spend quality time with her family. She and Alexis Bellino have always been the best of friends, but their friendship changed since Peggy joined the cast. They are having their ups and downs in their friendship, as Alexis found out that Peggy used to date her husband Jim Bellino. The tension between them was growing in each episode. Along with losing a good friend, Peggy and her husband Micah were losing their home as well. The couple eventually faced foreclosure and has legal issues with three banks.

Peggy has been suffering from postpartum depression. She has even attempted suicide. She speaks about this in a documentary with an actress friend.
