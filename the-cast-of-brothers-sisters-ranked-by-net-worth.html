The Cast Of 'Brothers &amp; Sisters' Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 5, 2024

Quick Links

  • Emily VanCamp - $8 Million
  • Rachel Griffiths - $8 Million
  • Matthew Rhys - $12 Million
  • Patricia Wettig - $30 Million
  • Calista Flockhart - $30 Million
  • Sally Field - $55 Million
  • Rob Lowe - $85 Million
  • Balthazar Getty - $200 Million

Brother & Sisters is a popular drama series that aired from 2006 to 2011 for 5 Seasons on ABC. The TV show revolved around the story of the wealthy Walker family and their lives in Los Angeles and Pasadena, California, after the death of the head of the family, William Walker. The TV show opened to rave reviews and became an instant hit. It has been nominated for various accolades, including Emmys and Golden Globes.

The TV show had an ensemble cast including Sally Field, Calista Flockhart, Rob Lowe, Emily VanCamp, etc. Below is a list comparing the net worth of all the cast members of Brother & Sisters.

Emily VanCamp - $8 Million

Emily VanCamp is a Canadian actress who became famous after her role in The WB drama Everwood, followed by her appearance as Rebecca Harper in Brother & Sisters. She gained prominence after appearing as Emily Thorne on the ABC series Revenge. Emily debuted in the MCU as Agent Sharon Stone in Captain America: The Winter Soldier, subsequently reprising her role in Captain America: Civil War and the Disney+ series The Falcon and the Winter Soldier. Following a successful TV career and a lucrative stint with the MCU, Emily has garnered a net worth of $8 Million.

Rachel Griffiths - $8 Million

Academy Award-nominated actress Rachel Griffiths plays the lead role of Sarah Walker in Brothers & Sisters. She is an acclaimed Australian actress known for her compelling roles in TV shows like Muriel's Wedding, Six Feet Under and for movies like Amy, My Best Friend's Wedding alongside Julia Roberts and Hilary and Jackie, which earned her an Oscar Nominations for Best Supporting Actress. Rachel has been the mainstay in Brothers & Sisters alongside several other TV shows earning her an $8 Million fortune.

Matthew Rhys - $12 Million

Matthew Rhys is an Emmy-winning actor who debuted in the 1997 movie House of America. His role as Kevin Walker in Brother & Sisters in 2006 brought him to the limelight and made him a star. Since then, he has appeared in various award-winning TV shows like The Americans, which earned him an Emmy Award and two Golden Globe nominations. He continued his excellent run with HBO’s Perry Mason, bagging him yet another Golden Globe nomination. With his amazing career in TV shows and movies, Matthew Rhys earned a net worth of $12 Million.

Patricia Wettig - $30 Million

Yet another Emmy-winning actress in the cast of Brothers & Sisters, Patricia Wettig, plays the role of Holly Harper in the show. Patrcia had her breakthrough role in 1987 with the TV show Thirtysomething and went on to star in several iconic TV shows like Courthouse and Prison Break. She starred in films like Guilty by Suspicion, City Slickers, etc. She is married to well-known producer Ken Olin and has an impressive $30 Million net worth.

Calista Flockhart - $30 Million

Calista Flockhart rose to fame with her role in the 1997 Fox television series Ally McBeal which earned her a Golden Globe Award. She further went on to star in TV shows like Brothers & Sisters, playing Kitty Walker and in Supergirl as Cat Grant. Calista’s major movie appearances include The Birdcage, A Midsummer Night's Dream, Things You Can Tell Just by Looking at Her, etc. She is currently married to legendary actor Harrison Ford and boasts a net worth of $30 Million, as published in Wealthy Gorilla.

Sally Field - $55 Million

Probably the most renowned actress in the cast of Brother & Sisters, Sally Field is a legendary 2-time Academy Award-winning actress. She plays the lead role of Nora Walker in Brother & Sisters, which is just a small part of her 5-decade-long illustrious career. Her best works include her roles in The Girl with Something Extra, Sybil, Norma Rae, Forrest Gump, The Amazing Spider-Man, etc., to name a few. Sally Field, with her 50-year-old career in Hollywood, has earned a mighty net worth of $55 Million as per The Richest, and she also owns a $2.3 million estate in Pacific Palisades, LA.

Rob Lowe - $85 Million

The second richest cast member of Brother & Sisters, Rob Lowe is an Emmy-nominated actor and filmmaker who plays the role of Robert McCallister. Lowe started acting at the age of 15 in the 1979 TV show A New Kind of Family and has since appeared in a plethora of movies and TV roles. His most notable work includes The Outsiders, About Last Night..., The West Wing, and Parks and Recreation. Rob Lowe has an excellent $85 Million net worth, as per Wealthy Genius. In 2005 Rob Lowe sold his Montecito home for a whopping $25 million and listed another of his properties for $42.5 million in 2018.

Balthazar Getty - $200 Million

Balthazar Getty is the richest cast member of Brother & Sisters and the great-grandson of J. Paul Getty, the founder of the famous Getty Oil, one of the richest men in the world during his time, as reported by The Things. Along with being the heir of the Getty empire, Balthazar is also an actor and musician mostly known for Lord of the Flies, Lost Highway, Alias, and Brothers And Sisters, playing the role of Tommy Walker. Balthazar Getty owing to his family business and career in Hollywood, has accumulated an awe-inducing net worth of $200 Million.

Apart from being excellent actors, the cast also has impressive net worths earning all the big bucks from their decorated careers in the industry. Balthazar Getty stands out as the richest cast member with a staggering fortune of $200 Million, easily beating all his co-actors by a huge margin.
