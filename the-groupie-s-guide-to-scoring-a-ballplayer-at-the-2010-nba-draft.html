The Groupies Guide To Scoring A Ballplayer At The 2010 NBA Draft

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Lives will be changed forever tonight at the 2010 NBA Draft. Sixty basketball players from the college and international ranks will hear their names called and walk across the stage at the Theatre at Madison Square Garden in New York City. And for many of them, it’ll be the culmination of a lifetime of hard work, perseverance, and sacrifice.

But those 60 guys aren’t the only ones who will see their dreams come true. If VH1’s hit show Basketball Wives taught us anything, it’s that somewhere out there, there’s a girl waiting to make her mark in the league, too. Only she’s hoping to do it by getting her hands onto a piece of the NBA’s freshest meat (a-yo!). If you’re planning on being one of those lucky ladies, there’s some stuff you should learn before you try and land yourself a baller at the 2010 NBA Draft. Here are 10 things you’ll need to know in order to turn one of this year’s lottery picks into your own personal lottery ticket. —Chris Yuscavage
