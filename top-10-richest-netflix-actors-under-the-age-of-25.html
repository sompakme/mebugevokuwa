Streaming service Netflix has proven to be a winner with many families and generations, whether young or old. And what's become more popular are the Netflix Original series' and movies.

From 13 Reasons Why to The Perfect Date, there are so many addicting shows and movies to binge on. The revolution of the Netflix and chill phenomenon has inevitably become a norm. Netflix has been knocking it out of the park with original films as well.

What's helped make this revolution is some of the compelling and talented young Netflix actors and actresses appearing in them. There is such great young talent out there. Let's take a look at the top 1o richest Netflix actors under the age of 25.

Shannon Purser: Worth $500,000

Shannon Purser is famous for her role as Sierra Burgess in the Netflix movie Sierra Burgess Is A Loser. She was co-stars with Noah Centineo on the film as well.

The 22-year old actress is from Atlanta Georgia, USA and she is also well-known for her role as Barb Holland in the Netflix show Stranger Things, which came out in 2016. In addition, she has also made her debut in the show Riverdale as Ethel Muggs.

Her estimated net worth is approximately $500,000. Shannon Purser has excelled as a TV actress and is sure to go far.

Jacob Elordi: Worth $1,000,000

The 22-year-old stunner Jacob Elordi is an Australian actor who has flourished in the Netflix comedy The Kissing Booth as character Noah Flynn. This movie was filmed in South Africa and one of his co-stars in the film was Joey King who has also made this list.

He also dated Joey King in 2018. The release of The Kissing Booth 2 is set to come out in 2020. Jacob's net worth is approximately $1 million USD. Let's face it we know that Jacob Elordi has stolen the hearts of many young female teens.

Noah Centineo: Worth $1,5 - $2,000,000

What's there not to love about Noah? Forget about the fact that he has a lovable babyface, but he has also become a Netflix sensation. Noah has been in a number of Netflix movies and series such as The Perfect Date, To All the Boys I've Loved Before, and Sierra Burgess is a Loser.

It comes as no surprise then that Noah has earned a lot of dough at the age of 23, after beginning his acting career in 2009. Noah Centineo's net worth is estimated at around $1.5 to 2 million and that number is just continuing to grow.

Florence Pugh: Worth $1 - $5,000,000

At just 23 years old, Florence Pugh has made quite a career for herself as an English actress. Florence Pugh was born in England on January 3, 1996. Some of her well-known movies and series on Netflix consist of Outlaw King, where she has starred as Elizabeth de Burgh, Marchella, and Malevolent.

KJ Apa: Worth $3,000,000

KJ Apa is an actor who has been applauded for remarkable acting skills. the "KJ" stands for Keneti James Fitzgerald. He is 22 years old from New Zealand who has risen to fame through his role in Riverdale as Archie Andrews and Griffin Hourigan in the Netflix comedy The Last Summer.

Kiernan Shipka: Worth $3,000,000

Acting for this young lady is nothing new as she started her acting career at the age of 5 months. She is the youngest actress on our list and has a net worth of about $3 million.

She has really made her platform with Netflix. Shipka is notable for her role as Sabrina Spellman in the Netflix series Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. In addition, she is well-known for her role as Aly Andreas in the horror Netflix movie The Silence. According to Den of Geek, "The Netflix movie The Silence showcases a family’s struggle to survive in a world overrun by carnivorous bats."

Joey King: Worth $3,000,000

Joey King started her acting career at the age of six years old. As mentioned, Joey King has been co-stars and has dated Jacob Elordi in the Netflix film The Kissing Booth. She is also friends with two actresses on our list; Katherine Langford and Florence Pugh. But her Netflix expedition doesn't end there.

Katherine Langford: Worth $5,000,000

Katherine Langford is no newbie when it comes to Netflix actresses. According to Film Zone, "Katherine has gotten big praise for her depiction of the American secondary school understudy Hannah Baker in 13 Reasons Why."

Tom Holland: Worth $4,000,000

Bella Thorne: Worth $5,000,000
