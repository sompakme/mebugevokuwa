What Joey Tribbiani's Exes Look Like Today

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Matt LeBlanc's character is everyone’s all-time favorite character from Friends. His charming smile, funny persona and good looks made viewers easily fall in love with him. Joey’s smooth pickup lines and job as a soap opera star landed him dates with some stunning women. However, not all his girlfriends were good for him, like his stalker ex, Erika, who was mentally unstable.

While most of his relationships were doomed from the start, some of Joey’s romances were good for him. Joey has the most entertaining love life among all the Friends characters' relationships. He’s dated models, actresses and some unique characters, and they were all played by gorgeous actresses who have gone on to do great work in the film industry. So, what do Joey Tribbiani’s exes look like today?

11 Joey Tribbiani’s Crazy Ex, Erika

Played By: Brooke Shields

  • Season: 2
  • Episode: The One After the Superbowl: Part 1

As a famous soap star, Joey Tribbiani has a lot of fans but proves why celebrities shouldn’t date their fans. Brooke Shields plays Erika, an obsessive fan of Joey’s character Dr. Drake. He dated her but soon regrets it after realizing she is mentally unwell.

Erika believes Joey and his Days of Our Lives character are the same person. Joey and Erika break up after he tricks her into accepting his show’s character is in Salem, and she leaves. Shields is still guest starring in popular television shows, with her most recent roles in Scream Queens and 9-1-1. This year, she released a two-part documentary in 2023 that follows her life titled Pretty Baby: Brooke Shields.

10 One Of Joey Tribbiani’s Sweetest Ex Girlfriends, Melanie

Played By: Corinne Bohrer

  • Season: 1
  • Episode: The One Where Rachel Finds Out

One of Joey Tribbiani’s best exes was Melanie. She and Tribbiani dated for a short period, during which she would fill his house with fruit gift baskets. Joey was going through a fertility study during their relationship, which meant he couldn’t have sex. However, that didn’t affect their relationship as much as it should have.

Melanie was played by Corinne Bohrer. Joey and her character never really broke up; she never returned to Friends after the sex study ended. Bohrer has since guest starred in popular shows like Veronica Mars and CW’s The Flash and still looks great.

9 Joey Tribbiani’s Girlfriend, Lauren

Played By: Jennifer Milmore

  • Season: 3
  • Episode: The One With The Tiny T-Shirt
  • Episode: The One With The Dollhouse

Lauren was Kate’s understudy at the theater where they worked after the latter was fired from Days of Our Lives. Joey initially used Lauren to make Kate jealous, but they still went on a few dates. Lauren was a huge fan of Joey’s TV show, which turned off Joey, so they broke up. Jennifer Milmore starred as Joey’s immature ex and has since featured in a few other television shows like Jesse.

8 Joey Tribbiani’s Girlfriend, Janine

Played By: Elle Macpherson

  • Season: 6
  • Episode: The One Where Phoebe Runs
  • Episode: The One With Ross' Teeth
  • Episode: The One Where Ross Got High
  • Episode: The One With The Routine
  • Episode: The One With The Apothecary Table

Australian bombshell Janine, played by Elle Macpherson, starts as Joey’s roommate before they begin a romantic relationship. She was one of his best ex-girlfriends because of her positive effect on him. Janine made Joey better in many ways, but she didn’t like Monica and Chandler, so he broke up with her.

The Sports Illustrated Swimsuit model is most known for her role in Friends but has had a successful career in business. Aside from acting and modeling, Elle owns a lingerie line, a beauty line and a wellness company called WellCo.

7 Joey Tribbiani’s Brief Relationship With Rachel

Played By: Jennifer Aniston

  • Main Cast

Despite their brief relationship, Joey and Rachel made one of the cutest couples on the show. Most fans felt the two dating came out of nowhere, but Rachel and Joey were always friendly and had fun with each other. Things didn’t work out for the pair after they tried to get intimate, but Rachel always had love for Joey. She is one of his best friends and ex-girlfriends.

Jennifer Aniston’s character on Friends is excellent for Joey, even if they didn’t end up together. Aniston is currently starring as a news anchor on The Morning Show with Reese Witherspoon, and she looks great.

6 Joey Tribbiani’s Girlfriend, Ursula

Played By Lisa Kudrow

  • Season: 1
  • Episode: The One with Two Parts: Part 1
  • Episode: The One with Two Parts: Part 2

Joey dates Phoebe’s twin, Ursula, but she is one of his worst relationship experiences. Ursula is nothing like her sister and is always mean to Joey. Ultimately, Phoebe breaks up with Joey on her twin sister's behalf so he doesn’t keep thinking he has a chance.

Lisa Kudrow, who plays Phoebe and her twin sister, recently appeared in 2020’s Space Force and 2021’s Friends: The Reunion. Kudrow will also appear in the upcoming Taika Waititi series, Time Bandits.

5 Joey Tribbiani’s Mature Girlfriend, Cecilia Monroe

Played By: Susan Sarandon

  • Season: 7
  • Episode: The One With Joey's New Brain

Cecilia was one of Joey Tribbiani’s most mature girlfriends. She and Joey both starred in Days of Our Lives and hooked up after she found out her character was going to get killed off the show. Cecilia, Susan Sarandon plays, allowed Joey to experience a different kind of woman than he was used to. However, their relationship didn’t last long after she got another acting gig in Mexico. Sarandon starred in a 2022 FOX show about a country music star, Monarch.

4 Joey Tribbiani’s Ex-Girlfriend, Kathy

Played By: Paget Brewster

  • Season: 4
  • Episode: The One With Joey's New Girlfriend
  • Episode: The One With The Dirty Girl
  • Episode: The One Where Chandler Crosses The Line

After so much heartbreak in season four of Friends, Joey Tribbiani thought he had finally met a good woman with Kathy, but that wasn’t the case. Paget Brewster’s character dates Joey, but she has stronger feelings for Chandler and kisses him. Joey and Chandler’s friendship almost doesn’t survive after this.

Kathy goes on to date Chandler but cheats on him, too, with her co-star in a play. She could be one of Joey’s worst ex-girlfriends. Aside from her guest role on the sitcom, Brewster is most known for her role as Emily Prentiss in Criminal Minds. She currently stars in the leading role in HBO’s Birdgirl.

3 Joey Tribbiani’s Ex, Charlie Wheeler

Played By: Aisha Tyler

  • Season: 9
  • Episode: The One with the Soap Opera Party
  • Episode: The One With the Fertility Test
  • Episode: The One With the Donor
  • Episode: The One in Barbados: Part 1
  • Episode: The One in Barbados: Part 2

One of Joey Tribbiani’s smartest and nicest exes was Charlie. She first met Ross and seemed to have more things in common with him than Joey. Still, they dated but broke up when Charlie found out Joey wasn’t smart enough to keep up with her. Charlie ended up dating Ross for a while before leaving him for her ex-boyfriend, a Nobel prize winner.

Aisha Tyler has an impressive acting career and has gone on to direct. She has done work on Roswell, New Mexico, and The Walking Dead. Tyler recently starred in the Apple TV + The Last Thing He Told Me with Jennifer Garner.

2 Joey Tribbiani’s Girlfriend, Katie Miller

Played By: Dina Meyer

  • Season: 3
  • Episode: The One With The Tiny T-Shirt
  • Episode: The One With The Screamer

Kate Miller, played by Dina Meyer, was Joey Tribbiani’s girlfriend on season 3 of Friends; the two worked together before she was fired from Days of Our Lives. Kate and Joey were constantly arguing, and she broke his heart. As soon as Kate got an offer to act in General Hospital, she took it and moved, leaving Joey.

Meyer guest starred in three Friends episodes before going on to other shows, Secret Agent Man, and most recently had guest starring roles in NCIS: Los Angeles, The Magicians and All American as Gwen Adams.

1 Joey Tribbiani's Girlfriend, Erin

Played By: Kristin Davis

  • Season: 7
  • Episode: The One With Ross' Library Book

Kristin Davis' character Erin and Joey Tribbiani were a casual hookup that turned into a serious relationship. She was the perfect girl for Joey. Erin was likable and liked to drink beer and eat sandwiches. But just as Joey developed real feelings for her, she lost interest in him after a few dates leading to their breakup. She told Phoebe and Rachel, “I don’t see this having a future.”

Davis told TODAY that she had a magical time appearing on the sitcom all those years ago. “It was a lot of fun just to be there and sit on the couch and coffee shop. It was amazing and fun.” Davis is set to appear in an upcoming action film alongside John Travolta.
