Arch manning – know his Age and Height

Arch manning is -year-old born in 2005. He is currently living in his hometown New Orleans, Louisiana.

Manning owns quite the requisite frame with a good height of 6 ft 4 in.

His listed weight is 215 lb (98 kg).

He made his mark on the football scene before high school when highlights of his passing became viral on Twitter. He is known for his excellent throwing on the move, effective throws across his body on the run, quick, smooth, repeatable delivery, and for always being on top of the ball especially, in the short-to-intermediate passing game. Considered as an elite QB prospect among the nation’s top recruits in the 2023 class, he might be a multi-year impact starter at the Power Five level, according to the recruiting analyst.

Who are Arch Manning parents?

Arch Manning is the son of Cooper Manning and his wife, whose name is not mentioned. Manning is the grandson of former New Orleans Saints quarterback Archie Manning, and the nephew of NFL legends Peyton and Eli Manning. Considering these facts he is regarded as a football royalty on bloodlines alone.

However, his dad Cooper, who was an all-star wide receiver in high school, was forced to retire from the sport after being diagnosed with spinal stenosis. However, he has managed to live a fine life working as an anchor of Fox Sports’ The Manning Hour and a director of investor relations at AJ Capital Partners.

According to Archie Manning, arch manning is well ahead of both of his uncles since, Peyton and Eli Manning had to wait until their sophomore seasons to take over as lead quarterback at Newman, while, Manning began his freshman season as the starting quarterback.
