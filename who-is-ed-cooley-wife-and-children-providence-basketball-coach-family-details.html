Ed Cooley Wikipedia Explored

Ed Cooley is the current head coach of the Providence Collegiate Friars men’s basketball team and an American college basketball coach. From 2006 until 2011, Cooley had the same post at Fairfield University. He was named the first Ben Jobe National Coach of the Year in 2010, an award given yearly to the best minority men’s college basketball coach in the country.

Cooley was born in Providence, Rhode Island, on September 10, 1969, to Jane Cooley and Edward Smith. In a low-income South Providence area, he was one of nine children raised by his mother in a welfare home. Cooley was eventually taken in by neighbors Gloria and Eddie Searight, who fed him and gave him a place to sleep.
