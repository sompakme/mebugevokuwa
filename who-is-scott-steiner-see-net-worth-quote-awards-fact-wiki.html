Who is Scott Steiner? See Net-worth, Quote, Awards, Fact, Wiki

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 27, 2024

Scott Carl Rechsteiner Wiki Biography

Scott Steiner was born Scott Carl Johnson Rechsteiner on 29 July 1962 in Bay City, Michigan. He is a professional wrestler best known for his fights in World Champion Wrestling. He has also wrestled for Total Nonstop Wrestling, Extreme Championship Wrestling, World Wrestling Federation/ Entertainment, and Jim Crockett Promotions. Besides Scott Steiner, some of his other ring names include Steiner Tiger and Scott Rechsteiner.

What’s the net worth of Scott Steiner? How rich is he, as of early 2016? According to sources, Scott Steiner has a net worth estimated to be more than $1 million. Among other accolades, he has under his belt three-time World Champion, one-time World Heavyweight Champion, two-time WCW heavyweight Champion, one-time TNA World Tag team Champion, and two-time World Television Champion. These titles have seen him earn more popularity and add to his net worth.

Scott Steiner Net Worth $1 Million

Scott Steiner started as an armature wrestler, wrestling at the University of Michigan where he schooled. As a freshman at the University, he managed to finish fifth, before being ranked sixth nationwide in his senior year. Scott decided to take his passion a notch higher, becoming a professional wrestler in 1986. He started well, defeating The Great Wojo to win the WWA World Heavyweight Championship on 14 August 1986 in the city of Dearborn, Michigan. In 1988, he joined Continental Wrestling Association, forming a tag team with fellow wrestler Billy Joe Travis to win CWA World Tag Team Championship after defeating Cuban Choir Boys on May 29 that same year.

From 1988 to 1992, he joined World Championship Wrestling together with his brother, Rick Steiner, and won the NWA World Tag Team Championship on 1 November 1989. They left and joined World Wrestling Foundation, where they wrestled until 1994. On 11 January 1993, he appeared on the very first episode or Raw, before moving to Extreme Championship Wrestling in 1995. Here he defeated wrestlers like Vampire Warrior (Gangrel), Dudley Dudley and Stevie Richards. In 1996, he returned to WCW, where he defeated Harlem Heat to win the World Tag Team Championship. He stayed there for five years, and in 2001 he moved to World Wrestling All-Stars until 2002. From 2010 to present he remains as independent circuit, and continues to win many fights and titles that have greatly contributed to his net worth.

In his personal life, Scott Steiner married Christa Podsedly in 2000 and the couple has two children. He lives with his wife in Detroit, Michigan. Over his career as a wrestler, he has had to deal with many controversies, which include multiple assault charges and alleged steroid abuse. In 2015, he was banned by WWE Hall of Fame officials following accusations by Hulk Hogan’s wife that he had accosted her and threatened to kill Hulk Hogan.
