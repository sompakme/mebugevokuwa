WWE Super Fan Frank The Clown Makes Night Of Champions Predictions For WrestleRumble.com, Best Of CS

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 1, 2024

Frank The Clown

One of WWE’s biggest fans, Frank The Clown, shot a new video where he gives some of his predictions for WWE Night of Champions that he’s submitting to WrestleRumble.com.

WrestleRumble.com allows you to predict who will win each match as well as other questions regarding the show. Each question is worth a certain amount of points. The top people with the most points wins prizes. $400 for first place. Followed by, $100 from ProWrestlingTees.com, $50 off, the new Daniel Bryan book and more. 

WrestleRumble.com has paid out thousands of dollars already with their most successful and attended pool being SummerSlam last month.
